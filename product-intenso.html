Sant'Emiliano Intenso

$19 / 250ml

Our most powerful expression — made entirely from 100% Moraiolo olives, a variety renowned across Umbria for its bold, assertive character. Pressed from olives harvested slightly later in the season when the fruit is at its most complex, Intenso delivers a rich, layered flavor of wild herbs, ripe olive fruit, and dark spice, followed by a long, warming finish that lingers on the palate. Designed for use raw — a drizzle over a ribollita, grilled bistecca, or a plate of aged pecorino transforms the dish entirely.
