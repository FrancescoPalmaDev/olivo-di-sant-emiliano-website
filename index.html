Our Story

From Our Grove
to Your Table

Nestled among the rolling hills of Umbria, our family has been cultivating olives for over a decade. We believe that truly great olive oil begins long before the mill — it starts with healthy, lovingly tended trees.

We harvest by hand at peak ripeness, mill within hours, and ship directly to your door — no middlemen, no compromises, complete traceability from tree to bottle. This is what real EVOO tastes like.

Why Olivo di Sant'Emiliano

The Olivo di Sant'Emiliano Difference

Single Estate

Every bottle comes from our own grove — no blending with oils from other farms or countries. Pure Umbrian terroir in every drop.

Hand Harvested

Harvested by hand at peak ripeness using traditional methods that have been refined over generations of Umbrian farming.

Pressed Within 24h

Olives travel directly from our grove to our partner mill within 24 hours of harvest — preserving peak freshness and polyphenols.

Direct to You

We ship directly from Umbria to your door — cutting out every middleman so you get fresher oil at a fairer price.

How It's Made

Grove to Bottle

A simple, honest process — nothing added, nothing hidden.

01
Tending the Grove

Year-round care of our 500+ olive trees using sustainable, low-impact farming practices that respect the land.

02
Hand Harvest

Olives are handpicked at optimal ripeness each autumn — never machine-beaten, never rushed.

03
Cold Press

Within 24 hours of picking, olives are cold-pressed using modern low-impact technology to protect flavor and nutrients.

04
Direct Delivery

Bottled, sealed, and shipped directly to you in the US — fully traceable from our grove to your kitchen.
